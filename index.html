Swipe & Direct Sticker Dragging

You can rotate cube layers directly with your cursor or finger! Just click/tap on any sticker, hold, and drag in the direction you want to turn. The app will calculate the correct slice and execute the rotation relative to your camera view.

Fully Unlocked 3D Camera Angles

To orbit, rotate, and view your Rubik's cube from absolutely any angle, simply drag your mouse or slide your finger on the **empty background space** outside the cube boundary. Double-click or click "Reset Camera" at any time to return to home perspective.

Keyboard Speedcubing Shortcuts

Press these keys on your physical keyboard to turn corresponding faces clockwise:

U Up (White)
D Down (Yellow)
L Left (Orange)
R Right (Red)
F Front (Green)
B Back (Blue)

💡 **Protip:** Hold **Shift** while pressing any of these keys to perform counter-clockwise prime turns (e.g., Shift + R runs $R'$).
